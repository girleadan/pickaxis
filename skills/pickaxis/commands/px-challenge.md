---
description: Suggest a stretch challenge for the developer's weakest skill axis.
---

1. Call `challenge_suggest`. It returns the developer's weakest axis and a question targeting it.
2. Frame the question as an exercise, not a quiz — invite the developer to reason out loud or sketch a design. Do not lecture.
3. When they attempt it, grade it as if it were `assess_answer`. Be honest about partial credit.
4. After grading, suggest ONE next step they could take this week to keep growing in that axis (read a specific doc, refactor a specific file, write a specific test). Make the suggestion small and concrete.
